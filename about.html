About Us


Bob has been hand painting models cars since the early 70s. He used to paint the old super modifieds that ran at San Jose, Clovis, Kearney Nowl in Fresno, and Madera. He then went into the U.S. Air Force and served from 1974-1978. In 1985, he started to paint hat pins and earings for sprint cars, at Baylands Raceway in Fremont. In fall of 1987, he set up the booth for the first time at the World of Outlaw event at Calistoga. At that time he sold custom hand-painted hat pins, earings, wooden and die cast sprint cars, and silhouette sprint car stickers. From 1987 to 1997 Bob sold at various events at Chico CA, Antioch CA, Tulare CA, Hanford CA, Placerville CA, Grass Valley CA, Ventura Ca, Perris Ca, El Centro CA, Cottage Grove OR, Skagit Dirt Cup, WA, Manzanita AZ, Jayhawk Nationals in Topeka KS, and Knoxville Nationals IA..
