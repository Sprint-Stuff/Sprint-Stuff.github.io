About Us


Bob has been Custom Hand Painting Models Cars since the early 70s. He used to paint the old Super Modifieds that ran at San Jose, Clovis, Kearney Bowl in Fresno, and Madera. He served in the USAF from '74-'78.

In '85, Bob started to Custom Hand-Paint Hat Pins and Earrings for Sprint Cars, at Baylands Raceway in Fremont, Ca.. In the fall of '87, the booth for the first time was set up at the WOO event at Calistoga. At that time Bob sold Custom Hand-Painted Hat Pins, Earrings, Wooden and Die Cast Sprint Cars, and silhouette Sprint Car Stickers.

From '87 to '97 Bob sold at various events at Chico, Antioch, Tulare, Hanford, Sacremento Mile, Placerville, Grass Valley, Oildale, Ventura, Perris, El Centro all in CA., Cottage Grove, OR., Skagit Dirt Cup, WA., Manzanita, AZ., Jayhawk Nationals in Topeka, KS.. Throughout the '90's, Bob's two oldest Step-Son's and Wife helped at the various events with running the family buisness including at the Knoxville Nationals, IA..

In the mid-'90s, Bob had a Son and Daughter of his own in which he spent his time Coaching Little League and Soccer and raise his Kids. In 2010, after the time away Bob started the Custom Hand Painting again and first set up again in Calistoga.

New products that were designed started with the Glow in the Dark Clothing Line (Wing, and Non-Wing) which includes Sweatshirts, Long-Sleeve T-shirts, T-shirts, Women's Tank Tops, and Women's V-necks, Kid's Shirts.

Our newest line are our Metal Products which include the 3D Hitch Covers (Wing and Non-Wing) with LED back light, Hangers (Wing and Non-Wing) for Keys/Coats/Hats/Kitchen-Utensils/etc.
