About Us


Bob has been Custom Hand Painting Models Cars since the early 70s. He used to paint the old Super Modifieds that ran at San Jose, Clovis, Kearney Bowl in Fresno, and Madera. He served in the USAF from '74-'78.

In '85, Bob started to Custom Hand-Paint Hat Pins and Earrings for Sprint Cars, at Baylands Raceway in Fremont, Ca.. In the fall of '87, the booth for the first time was set up at the WOO event at Calistoga. At that time Bob sold Custom Hand-Painted Hat Pins, Earrings, Wooden and Die Cast Sprint Cars, and silhouette Sprint Car Stickers. From '87 to '97 Bob sold at various events at Chico, CA., Antioch, CA., Tulare, CA., Hanford, CA., Placerville, CA., Grass Valley, CA., Ventura, CA., Perris, CA., El Centro, CA., Cottage Grove, OR., Skagit Dirt Cup, WA., Manzanita, AZ., Jayhawk Nationals in Topeka, KS., and Knoxville Nationals, IA.. Throughout the '90's Bob's two oldest son's and wife helped at the various events with running the family buisness.
